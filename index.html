Hidden Prints

Discover the under-represented craft of Sanganer & Bagru block printing — explore, play, and collect badges.

Why this craft matters

Rajasthan's block printing — especially from Sanganer and Bagru — is an intricate, community-driven craft. Tourists often see finished garments, but not the dye vats, the family-run kilns, or the apprentices learning patterns from elders. This site pulls back the curtain through short facts and a small game.
